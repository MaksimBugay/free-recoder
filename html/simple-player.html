Live Streaming with Pushca Demo

1. Open the recorder page on a remote machine.

2. On the player page, click the "Play recording" button.

3. On the recorder page, click the "Start recording" button.

4. Begin your performance: talk, sing, or dance.

5. The live stream will start on the player page with a delay of approximately 15-30 seconds.

6. Once you are satisfied with the demo, click the "Stop recording" button on the recorder page.
